Clove Cards

Clove Cards are Smart-QR code accessed cryptocurrency wallets that connect crypto users, developers, influencers, and local leaders through Blockchain networks. These physical wallets allow users to connect to blockchain networks and start engaging with communities simply by scanning a QR Code. They are printed on high-quality plastic or paper cards- one side a One-Scan QR code that launches a user-created public profile, the other side, a private One-Scan Smart-QR code that launches an in-browser web app where users access all of the features of a cryptocurrency wallet.

  • Requires no download or hardware
  • Includes ready-to-use Blockchain Accounts
  • Connects to Decentralized Apps and Exchanges
  • Disburse crypto assets in bulk to any or all physical cards printed
